Introduction

Home battery prediction and automatic charging for Home Assistant supporting multiple inverters including:

  • GivEnergy Hybrid, AC and AIO (Local & Cloud)
  • Solis
  • Solax (Local & Cloud)
  • Sunsynk
  • Huawei
  • SolarEdge
  • Fox (Local & Cloud)
  • Sofar
  • LuxPower
  • Solar Assistant
  • Sigenergy Sigenstor

Also known by some as Batpred or Batman!
